RELATIONSHIPS

//ONE TO ONE//
Adopter To Pet-Adopted | Each Adopter gets One Pet and each Pet can have One Adopter. 

//ONE TO MANY//
Pet Type to Pet Breed | One Pet Type can have many Breeds but One Pet Breed can have only Pet Type.

//MANY TO MANY//
Adopter to Pet-Type | Each Adopter can adopt many Pet Types, and different Pet-Types can have many/different Adopters
